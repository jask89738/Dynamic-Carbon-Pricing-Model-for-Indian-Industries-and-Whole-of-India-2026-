# Dynamic-Carbon-Pricing-Model-for-Indian-Industries-and-Whole-of-India-2026-
A comprehensive live research project developing a dynamic carbon pricing model for Indian industries under the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme (CCTS) 2026.

Jasleen Kaur
MBA Student — Sustainable Finance

This project sits at the intersection of technical modeling, financial analysis, and climate policy — developing actionable tools for India's historic transition to a formal carbon market. The research bridges academic rigor with practical application, delivering a working pricing model, scenario analyses, and an interactive dashboard for industry stakeholders and policymakers.

Project Objectives
Dynamic Carbon Pricing Model
Forecast carbon credit prices under India's CCTS framework incorporating floor/forbearance price mechanisms, supply-demand dynamics, and sector-specific compliance costs.

Live Market Simulation
Replicate upcoming power exchange-based trading of carbon credits, enabling scenario analysis of different compliance strategies and market conditions.

Sector-Specific Impact Analysis
Evaluate financial implications for each obligated sector — compliance costs, surplus credit revenue, and abatement investment breakeven points.

Strategic Recommendations
Actionable insights for Make-or-Buy decisions, CBAM preparedness, and long-term carbon management positioning for Indian industries.
